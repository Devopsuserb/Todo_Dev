The application

TO DO is developed to help users to track their tasks and the progress to make their daily life easy and better.
Everyone wants to achieve their tasks but sometimes it is hard to keep a track of them. To help users in tracking their tasks and working on achieving them we help. TO DO is designed simple as a Single Page Application design with special features.

What can you do?

My answer you can do a lot
  • The task table contains tasks marked in different colors the active tasks are in Blue and the completed tasks in Gold.
  • A search box is available to search from the tasks.
  • A drop down is provided to toggle from all records to completed/active tasks.
  • A add button is provided for adding new tasks. User can change the status of the task from active to completed or vice-versa.
  • User can either delete a single task or all the tasks at once
